Space Invaders

Defend the base, clear every wave, and press Space to fire.

Controls

Move with arrow keys or A/D. Fire with space. Press P to pause or resume. Press Enter to restart after a win or loss (also resumes when paused).

Goal

Clear all invaders before they reach the bottom. Each level speeds up the formation and increases pressure.
